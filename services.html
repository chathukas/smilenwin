Services

Our services at School are designed to help special needs students thrive. From individualized education plans to speech and occupational therapy, counseling, and adaptive physical education, we are dedicated to providing the support and accommodations necessary for every child to reach their full potential.

Why They

Choose Us

At Our School, we are committed to providing high-quality services and support to our students and their families. Our team of professionals is dedicated to helping every child reach their full potential, no matter their challenges. We offer a range of services to support our students' learning, development, and overall well-being.

We Offer

Individualized Education Plans

Every child at Our School receives an individualized education plan (IEP) tailored to their unique needs and abilities. Our team works closely with parents and caregivers to develop a plan that addresses their child's specific challenges and goals, and ensures that they receive the support and accommodations they need to succeed.

Occupational Therapy

Occupational therapy is an essential part of our approach to supporting our students' learning and development. Our licensed occupational therapists work with children to improve their fine motor skills, sensory processing, and other areas that can impact their academic and social success.

Speech Therapy

Our speech therapy services are designed to help children improve their communication skills and overcome speech and language disorders. Our licensed speech therapists work with children individually or in small groups to develop their language, articulation, and social communication skills.

Counseling

We recognize that social and emotional well-being is essential to our students' success. That's why we offer counseling services to support our students' mental health and emotional development. Our licensed counselors work with children and families to address a range of concerns, from anxiety and depression to social skills and behavior management.

Adaptive Physical Education

Physical education is an important part of our students' education and overall well-being. Our adaptive physical education program is designed to meet the unique needs of children with disabilities or other challenges, while also promoting physical activity, health, and fitness.
